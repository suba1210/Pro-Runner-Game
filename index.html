Welcome To Pro Runner!


Instructions to Play

  1. Pro Runner will start running when you start the game.
  2. When you see the holes, Click empty space or space bar of your computer to move the runner to the opposite side.
  3. If the runner touches the holes, the game will be lost.
  4. Your score will be based on the distance travelled by the runner.
  5. In Hacker Mode,
    • As the score increase, the runner will move faster and faster.
    • There will be obstacles moving up and down on the screen, If the runner touches those obstacles, the game will END.
    • There will power ups in the game area, When the runner touches the power ups, the game will be slowed down for some time.
